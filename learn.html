WHY THIS EXISTS

The city stepped back. We stepped up.

Ocala's recycling program got too expensive and the service fell apart. Instead of asking you to pay to recycle, we flipped it: you earn for it. The carrot works better than the stick.

Your material has real value. It was just going to someone else. Now it goes to you.

WHAT IS WTWR

Credits you earn, not a coin you gamble on.

When you recycle, you earn WTWR — a credit you redeem for discounts at local stores. That's it. It's a reward for good habits, like a punch card that works all over town.

A store list you can tap and go is coming soon. COMING SOON

HOW TO GET THE MOST

If it has metal, snap a photo.

Doing a remodel? Got car batteries, aluminum, an old sink, a dead fridge? Send a pic. We'll tell you if it's worth money, how to make it worth more, and whether to hold for a bigger load or send it now.

Sorting what needs sorting only helps you — a clean load is worth more than a mixed one.
