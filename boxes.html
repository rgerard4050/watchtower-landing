// How It Works

Four steps.
You do two of them.

We handle the logistics, the grading, the processing, and the payout. Your job is to fill the box and leave it out.

01
Request your box
Fill out the form below. We ship a free prepaid smart box to your address. Arrives flat. Takes 30 seconds to set up.
02
Fill it clean
Old phones, laptops, cables, circuit boards, metals, batteries. The cleaner and more complete, the higher your payout. We tell you exactly what we want.
03
Leave it out
Seal the box. Set it on your porch or loading dock. A driver picks it up or USPS takes it back — the prepaid label is already on the box. It scans into our system the moment it moves.
04
Get paid
Box arrives at our node. AI grades the contents. WTWR hits your account within 48 hours. Redeem for cash, fuel credit, or store credit at partner locations.
