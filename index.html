Why this exists

Ocala couldn't afford it.
So we built something better.

The city's recycling got too expensive and the service fell apart. We're trying a different way — one where you don't pay to recycle, you earn for it. The carrot works better than the stick.

You sort what needs sorting, and that only helps you. When you submit a full bounty, we verify it meets the minimum — whether it's one clean category or a mixed load.

No more rigid every-day, every-week pickup. This is à la carte: you cash in once you've got enough above the minimum. Doing a remodel? Got car batteries, aluminum scrap, an old sink, a dead fridge? Rule of thumb — if it has metal, snap a photo. We'll tell you if it's worth money, how to make it worth more, and whether to hold for a bigger load or send it now.
